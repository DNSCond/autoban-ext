## strikes format

in the name field put `(` and `)` with an integer between them as warnings until ban,
and then a comma and optionally a space andthen the duration in days, 0 for permanent and then a d.

## example

```
name: (17, 2d) spam
you were warned 17 times for spam and therefore abnned by 2 days
---
name: (9, 5d) spoilers
>!spoilerText isnt allowed!<
```
